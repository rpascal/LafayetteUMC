AWANA
Approved Workmen Are Not Ashamed Grade K - 8

Meet on Wednesday evenings from 6:00 to 8:00. (Starting September 7, 2016)

The evening starts with a good home cooked meal at 6:00 and time to hang out with old and new friends.

After Dinner the children are divided into four groups: Grades 1-2, Grades 3-4, Grades 5-6, Grades 7-8. Children are encouraged to memorize scripture and earn rewards based on their progress through their handbooks. Games complete the evening.
