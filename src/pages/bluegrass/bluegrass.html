Bluegrass
There is a lot of foot stomping and singing going on at The Lafayette UMC Bluegrass Jam on Friday nights! If you enjoy bluegrass music, come on in and see some of the best local bluegrass bands. Admission is by Donation, $3.00 suggested at the door. The Kitchen opens at 5:30 pm featuring slawdogs, hamburgers, sloppy joes, and more with suggested donation. Some of the desserts are homemade or bring your favorite dessert to share.
Are you in a Bluegrass Band and want to play? You need to get there early to get on the schedule. Music starts at 7:00 pm.
