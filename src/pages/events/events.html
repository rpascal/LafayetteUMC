Back to Church Sunday

Sunday, September 9

Come for our regular worship service at 11:00 a.m., where we will have a special recognition for our local first responders. Following the service, stay for an afternoon full of food, fun, and fellowship! We will begin with a potluck lunch. Hot dogs and sloppy joes will be provided. Bring a dish to share. Afterwards we will have a Corn Hole tournament (with prizes), water balloons, sidewalk chalk, sack races, and board games. Hope to see everyone there! Bring a friend!

Car Show

Saturday, October 13

10 a.m.- 2 p.m

On Saturday, October 13, from 10 a.m.- 2 p.m., we will be having a Car Show, coinciding with the Medina County Fall Foliage Tour. Come show off your fancy care for free. There will be free food available (hot dogs, potato salad, water). $1.00 to vote for your favorite car and the winter gets a trophy for Best of Show. Bring a chair to sit and chat. We are also hoping to have some live music playing. Hope to see you there!
